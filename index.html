Neighborhoods

Rainier Valley

In Rainier Valley, the neighborhood with the highest concentration of refugees is Rainier Beach. This area is known for its significant refugee population from Somalia, Vietnam, Ethiopia, and other countries.

Columbia City and Hillman City, also within Rainier Valley, are notable for their diverse populations, including many immigrants and refugees.

Tukwila

In Tukwila, the neighborhood with the highest population of refugees is the area around the Riverton Park United Methodist Church.

This location has become a significant hub for asylum seekers and refugees, particularly from countries like Venezuela, Angola, and Congo.
